Zombies Can't Read

An FPS game made for the Scream Roulette Game Jam.

Zombies Can't Read was made in just one week for the Scream Roulette Game Jam, where it competed against 64 other entries and came in 35th place. It was my first time ever actually finishing and submitting a jam game on time. It's a weird first-person survival shooter with an interesting points system. It is not my best work, but it holds a special place in my heart.
